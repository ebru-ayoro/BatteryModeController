Battery Mode Controller v1.0

A lightweight Windows WPF app that automatically switches your
power plan when you plug or unplug your laptop.

  Features
  - Manual mode: pick and apply any power plan
  - Auto mode: assign AC/battery plans, auto-switches on
    plug/unplug
  - Shows current AC status and battery percentage
  - Auto-detects Windows light/dark theme
  - Active plan indicator
  - Preferences saved between sessions
  - No background processes — close to exit

  Requirements
  Windows 10 or 11 with .NET 9.0 Runtime
